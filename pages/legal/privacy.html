Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Anubis Scripts ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data, use Google OAuth for authentication, and store everything locally in your browser. We don't sell your data or track you across the web.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you sign in with Google OAuth, we receive:

  • Google User ID: A unique identifier for your Google account
  • Display Name: Your name as shown on your Google account
  • Profile Picture: URL to your Google profile picture
  • Email Address: Only if you explicitly grant permission (optional)

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and features used
  • Device Information: Screen resolution and device type (desktop/mobile)

2.3 Scripts You Upload

If you upload scripts to the platform:

  • Script title, description, and code
  • Category and tags
  • Upload timestamp
  • Your user ID (to associate the script with your account)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the Service: Display your profile, manage uploads, and enable features
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Prevent Abuse: Detect and prevent spam, malicious uploads, and ToS violations
  • Communicate: Send important updates about the Service (rare, opt-in only)

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track you across other websites
  • Share your data with Roblox Corporation

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • SessionStorage: Authentication tokens and session data (cleared when you close the browser)
  • LocalStorage: User preferences and settings (persists across sessions)
  • Server Storage: Uploaded scripts and user profiles (if backend is implemented)

4.2 Security Measures

  • Google OAuth 2.0: Industry-standard authentication (we never see your password)
  • HTTPS: All data transmitted over encrypted connections
  • No Password Storage: We don't store passwords - Google handles authentication
  • Script Review: Uploaded scripts are reviewed for malicious content

4.3 Data Retention

  • Session Data: Cleared when you close the browser
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Uploaded Scripts: Retained indefinitely unless you delete them
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google OAuth

We use Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. When you sign in, Google's Privacy Policy applies to the authentication process. We only receive the information you authorize Google to share.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 GitHub

Our source code is hosted on GitHub. If you interact with our GitHub repository, GitHub's Privacy Policy applies.

5.3 Discord

Our community Discord server is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We do not control or access Discord's data collection.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and browser storage:

  • Authentication Token: Stored in sessionStorage to keep you logged in
  • User Preferences: Theme, language, and display settings in localStorage
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We don't use advertising or tracking cookies

You can clear cookies and storage at any time through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all data we have about you
  • Edit Your Profile: Update your display name and profile picture
  • Delete Your Account: Permanently remove your account and data
  • Export Your Data: Download all scripts you've uploaded

7.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Sign Out: Click "Sign Out" to end your session and clear session data
  • Revoke Google Access: Remove Anubis Scripts from your Google account permissions
  • Clear Browser Data: Delete cookies and storage through browser settings

8. Children's Privacy

Anubis Scripts is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

9. International Users

Anubis Scripts is accessible worldwide. If you access the Service from outside your country, your information may be transferred to and processed in different jurisdictions. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Provide details about what data was compromised
  • Explain steps we're taking to address the breach
  • Recommend actions you should take to protect yourself

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Discord: https://discord.gg/FeSD9YyA4r
  • GitHub: https://github.com/biokiller59

Summary: We collect minimal data, use Google OAuth for secure authentication, and store most data locally in your browser. We don't sell your data, track you across the web, or share information with third parties. You can delete your account and data at any time.
