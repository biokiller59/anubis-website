Get Your Key

Follow the steps below to get your free access key

ℹ️ Why do we use a key system?

The key system helps us keep the scripts free for everyone while covering server costs. It only takes 2-3 minutes to get a key, and it's valid for 24 hours.

How to Get Your Key

1

Visit Junky Developer

Go to Junky Developer link shortener to start the process. You'll need to complete a checkpoint to get your key.

2

Complete the Checkpoint

On Junky Developer, you'll see a checkpoint page. Click "I'm not a robot" and wait a few seconds for verification.

3

Click "Continue"

After the verification is complete, click the "Continue" or "Get Link" button to proceed to the next page.

4

Copy Your Key

You'll be redirected to a page showing your unique key. Copy this key - you'll need it when running the Anubis script.

5

Use Your Key in Script

Copy your unique key from the page. When you run the Anubis script in your executor, paste the key when prompted. The script will verify your key and grant you access for 24 hours!

⚠️ Important Notes

• Keys expire after 24 hours - You'll need to get a new key after expiration

• Don't share your key - Each key is unique and for personal use only

• Use an ad blocker - We recommend using an ad blocker for a better experience

• Having issues? - Join our Discord for help
